# 1 John 2:14 (New International Version)

    because you know the Father.  
I write to you, fathers,  
    because you know him who is from the beginning.<sup>^B</sup>

    because you are strong,<sup>^C</sup>  
    and the word of God<sup>^D</sup> lives in you,<sup>^E</sup>  
    and you have overcome the evil one.<sup>^F</sup>
