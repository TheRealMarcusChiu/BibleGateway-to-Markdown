# Acts 12:20 (New International Version)
He had been quarreling with the people of Tyre and Sidon;[^A] they now joined together and sought an audience with him. After securing the support of Blastus, a trusted personal servant of the king, they asked for peace, because they depended on the king's country for their food supply.[^B]
