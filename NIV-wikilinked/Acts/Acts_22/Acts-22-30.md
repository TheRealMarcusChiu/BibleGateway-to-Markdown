# Acts 22:30 (New International Version)
Paul Before the Sanhedrin
The commander wanted to find out exactly why Paul was being accused by the Jews.[^A] So the next day he released him[^B] and ordered the chief priests and all the members of the Sanhedrin[^C] to assemble. Then he brought Paul and had him stand before them.
