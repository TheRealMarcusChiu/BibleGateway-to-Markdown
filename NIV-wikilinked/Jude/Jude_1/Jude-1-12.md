# Jude 12 (New International Version)
These people are blemishes at your love feasts,<sup>^A</sup> eating with you without the slightest qualm--shepherds who feed only themselves.<sup>^B</sup> They are clouds without rain,<sup>^C</sup> blown along by the wind;<sup>^D</sup> autumn trees, without fruit and uprooted<sup>^E</sup>--twice dead.
