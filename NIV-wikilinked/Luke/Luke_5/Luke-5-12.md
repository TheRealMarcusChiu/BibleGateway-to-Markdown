# Luke 5:12 (New International Version)
Jesus Heals a Man With Leprosy<sup>^A</sup>
While Jesus was in one of the towns, a man came along who was covered with leprosy.<sup>^B</sup> When he saw Jesus, he fell with his face to the ground and begged him, "Lord, if you are willing, you can make me clean."
