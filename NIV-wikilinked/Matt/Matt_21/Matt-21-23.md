# Matthew 21:23 (New International Version)
The Authority of Jesus Questioned[^A]
Jesus entered the temple courts, and, while he was teaching, the chief priests and the elders of the people came to him. "By what authority[^B] are you doing these things?" they asked. "And who gave you this authority?"
