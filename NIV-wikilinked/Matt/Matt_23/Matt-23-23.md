# Matthew 23:23 (New International Version)
"Woe to you, teachers of the law and Pharisees, you hypocrites! You give a tenth<sup>^A</sup> of your spices--mint, dill and cumin. But you have neglected the more important matters of the law--justice, mercy and faithfulness.<sup>^B</sup> You should have practiced the latter, without neglecting the former.
