# Matthew 8:28 (New International Version)
Jesus Restores Two Demon-Possessed Men<sup>^A</sup>
When he arrived at the other side in the region of the Gadarenes, two demon-possessed<sup>^B</sup> men coming from the tombs met him. They were so violent that no one could pass that way.
