# Revelation 20:4 (New International Version)
I saw thrones[^A] on which were seated those who had been given authority to judge.[^B] And I saw the souls of those who had been beheaded[^C] because of their testimony about Jesus[^D] and because of the word of God.[^E] They had not worshiped the beast[^F] or its image and had not received its mark on their foreheads or their hands.[^G] They came to life and reigned[^H] with Christ a thousand years.
