# Romans 12:1 (New International Version)
A Living Sacrifice
Therefore, I urge you,<sup>^A</sup> brothers and sisters, in view of God's mercy, to offer your bodies as a living sacrifice,<sup>^B</sup> holy and pleasing to God--this is your true and proper worship.
