# Romans 12:3 (New International Version)
Humble Service in the Body of Christ
For by the grace given me<sup>^A</sup> I say to every one of you: Do not think of yourself more highly than you ought, but rather think of yourself with sober judgment, in accordance with the faith God has distributed to each of you.
