# Romans 4:11 (New International Version)
And he received circumcision as a sign, a seal of the righteousness that he had by faith while he was still uncircumcised.[^A] So then, he is the father[^B] of all who believe[^C] but have not been circumcised, in order that righteousness might be credited to them.
