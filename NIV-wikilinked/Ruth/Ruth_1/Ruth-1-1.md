# Ruth 1:1 (New International Version)
Naomi Loses Her Husband and Sons
In the days when the judges ruled,[^A] there was a famine in the land.[^B] So a man from Bethlehem in Judah,[^C] together with his wife and two sons, went to live for a while[^D] in the country of Moab.[^E]
