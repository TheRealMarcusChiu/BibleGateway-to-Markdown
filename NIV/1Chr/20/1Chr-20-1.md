# 1 Chronicles 20:1 (New International Version)
The Capture of Rabbah[^A]
In the spring, at the time when kings go off to war, Joab led out the armed forces. He laid waste the land of the Ammonites and went to Rabbah[^B] and besieged it, but David remained in Jerusalem. Joab attacked Rabbah and left it in ruins.[^C]
