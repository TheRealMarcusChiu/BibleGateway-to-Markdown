As for Jeduthun, from his sons:[^A]
Gedaliah, Zeri, Jeshaiah, Shimei, Hashabiah and Mattithiah, six in all, under the supervision of their father Jeduthun, who prophesied, using the harp[^B] in thanking and praising the LORD.
