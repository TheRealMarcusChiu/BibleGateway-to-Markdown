# 1 Chronicles 4:41 (New International Version)
The men whose names were listed came in the days of Hezekiah king of Judah. They attacked the Hamites in their dwellings and also the Meunites[^A] who were there and completely destroyed them, as is evident to this day. Then they settled in their place, because there was pasture for their flocks.
