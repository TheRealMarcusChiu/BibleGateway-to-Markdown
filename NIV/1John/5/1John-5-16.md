# 1 John 5:16 (New International Version)
If you see any brother or sister commit a sin that does not lead to death, you should pray and God will give them life.[^A] I refer to those whose sin does not lead to death. There is a sin that leads to death.[^B] I am not saying that you should pray about that.[^C]
