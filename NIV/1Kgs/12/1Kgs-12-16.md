# 1 Kings 12:16 (New International Version)
When all Israel saw that the king refused to listen to them, they answered the king:
"What share[^A] do we have in David,  
    what part in Jesse's son?  
To your tents, Israel![^B]  
    Look after your own house, David!"
So the Israelites went home.[^C]
