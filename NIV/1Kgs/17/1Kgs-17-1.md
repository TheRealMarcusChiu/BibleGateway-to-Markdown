# 1 Kings 17:1 (New International Version)
Elijah Announces a Great Drought
Now Elijah[^A] the Tishbite, from Tishbe in Gilead,[^B] said to Ahab, "As the LORD, the God of Israel, lives, whom I serve, there will be neither dew nor rain[^C] in the next few years except at my word."
