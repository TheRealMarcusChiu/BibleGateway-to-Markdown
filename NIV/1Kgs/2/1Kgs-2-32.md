# 1 Kings 2:32 (New International Version)
The LORD will repay[^A] him for the blood he shed,[^B] because without my father David knowing it he attacked two men and killed them with the sword. Both of them--Abner son of Ner, commander of Israel's army, and Amasa[^C] son of Jether, commander of Judah's army--were better[^D] men and more upright than he.
