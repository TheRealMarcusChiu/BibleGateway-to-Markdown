# 1 Kings 20:34 (New International Version)
"I will return the cities[^A] my father took from your father," Ben-Hadad[^B] offered. "You may set up your own market areas[^C] in Damascus,[^D] as my father did in Samaria."
Ahab said, "On the basis of a treaty[^E] I will set you free." So he made a treaty with him, and let him go.
