# 1 Kings 3:26 (New International Version)
The woman whose son was alive was deeply moved[^A] out of love for her son and said to the king, "Please, my lord, give her the living baby! Don't kill him!"
But the other said, "Neither I nor you shall have him. Cut him in two!"
