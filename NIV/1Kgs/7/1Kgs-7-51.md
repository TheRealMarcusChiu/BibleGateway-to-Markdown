# 1 Kings 7:51 (New International Version)
When all the work King Solomon had done for the temple of the LORD was finished, he brought in the things his father David had dedicated[^A]--the silver and gold and the furnishings[^B]--and he placed them in the treasuries of the LORD's temple.
