# 1 Samuel 14:45 (New International Version)
But the men said to Saul, "Should Jonathan die--he who has brought about this great deliverance in Israel? Never! As surely as the LORD lives, not a hair[^A] of his head will fall to the ground, for he did this today with God's help." So the men rescued[^B] Jonathan, and he was not put to death.
