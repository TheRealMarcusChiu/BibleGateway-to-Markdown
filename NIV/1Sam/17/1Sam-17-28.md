# 1 Samuel 17:28 (New International Version)
When Eliab, David's oldest brother, heard him speaking with the men, he burned with anger[^A] at him and asked, "Why have you come down here? And with whom did you leave those few sheep in the wilderness? I know how conceited you are and how wicked your heart is; you came down only to watch the battle."
