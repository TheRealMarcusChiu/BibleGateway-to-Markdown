# 1 Samuel 17:55 (New International Version)
As Saul watched David[^A] going out to meet the Philistine, he said to Abner, commander of the army, "Abner,[^B] whose son is that young man?"
Abner replied, "As surely as you live, Your Majesty, I don't know."
