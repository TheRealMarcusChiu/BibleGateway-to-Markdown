# 1 Samuel 2:25 (New International Version)
If one person sins against another, God may mediate for the offender; but if anyone sins against the LORD, who will[^A] intercede[^B] for them?" His sons, however, did not listen to their father's rebuke, for it was the LORD's will to put them to death.
