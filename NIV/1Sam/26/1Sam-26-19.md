# 1 Samuel 26:19 (New International Version)
Now let my lord the king listen[^A] to his servant's words. If the LORD has incited you against me, then may he accept an offering.[^B] If, however, people have done it, may they be cursed before the LORD! They have driven me today from my share in the LORD's inheritance[^C] and have said, 'Go, serve other gods.'[^D]
