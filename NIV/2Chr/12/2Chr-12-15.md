# 2 Chronicles 12:15 (New International Version)
As for the events of Rehoboam's reign, from beginning to end, are they not written in the records of Shemaiah[^A] the prophet and of Iddo the seer that deal with genealogies? There was continual warfare between Rehoboam and Jeroboam.
