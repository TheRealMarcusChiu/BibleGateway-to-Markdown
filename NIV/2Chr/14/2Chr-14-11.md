# 2 Chronicles 14:11 (New International Version)
Then Asa called[^A] to the LORD his God and said, "LORD, there is no one like you to help the powerless against the mighty. Help us,[^B] LORD our God, for we rely[^C] on you, and in your name[^D] we have come against this vast army. LORD, you are our God; do not let mere mortals prevail[^E] against you."
