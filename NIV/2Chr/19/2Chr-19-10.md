# 2 Chronicles 19:10 (New International Version)
In every case that comes before you from your people who live in the cities--whether bloodshed or other concerns of the law, commands, decrees or regulations--you are to warn them not to sin against the LORD;[^A] otherwise his wrath will come on you and your people. Do this, and you will not sin.
