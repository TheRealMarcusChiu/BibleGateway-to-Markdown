# 2 Chronicles 20:21 (New International Version)
After consulting the people, Jehoshaphat appointed men to sing to the LORD and to praise him for the splendor of his holiness[^A] as they went out at the head of the army, saying:
"Give thanks to the LORD,  
    for his love endures forever."[^B]
