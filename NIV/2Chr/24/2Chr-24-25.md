# 2 Chronicles 24:25 (New International Version)
When the Arameans withdrew, they left Joash severely wounded. His officials conspired against him for murdering the son of Jehoiada the priest, and they killed him in his bed. So he died and was buried[^A] in the City of David, but not in the tombs of the kings.
