# 2 Chronicles 27:5 (New International Version)
Jotham waged war against the king of the Ammonites[^A] and conquered them. That year the Ammonites paid him a hundred talents of silver, ten thousand cors of wheat and ten thousand cors of barley. The Ammonites brought him the same amount also in the second and third years.
