# 2 Chronicles 30:1 (New International Version)
Hezekiah Celebrates the Passover
Hezekiah sent word to all Israel[^A] and Judah and also wrote letters to Ephraim and Manasseh,[^B] inviting them to come to the temple of the LORD in Jerusalem and celebrate the Passover[^C] to the LORD, the God of Israel.
