# 2 Chronicles 34:22 (New International Version)
Hilkiah and those the king had sent with him went to speak to the prophet[^A] Huldah, who was the wife of Shallum son of Tokhath, the son of Hasrah, keeper of the wardrobe. She lived in Jerusalem, in the New Quarter.
