# 2 Chronicles 36:10 (New International Version)
In the spring, King Nebuchadnezzar sent for him and brought him to Babylon,[^A] together with articles of value from the temple of the LORD, and he made Jehoiachin's uncle, Zedekiah, king over Judah and Jerusalem.
