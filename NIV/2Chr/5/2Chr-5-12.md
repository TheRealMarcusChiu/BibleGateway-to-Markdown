# 2 Chronicles 5:12 (New International Version)
All the Levites who were musicians[^A]--Asaph, Heman, Jeduthun and their sons and relatives--stood on the east side of the altar, dressed in fine linen and playing cymbals, harps and lyres. They were accompanied by 120 priests sounding trumpets.[^B]
