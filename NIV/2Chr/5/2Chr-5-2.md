# 2 Chronicles 5:2 (New International Version)
The Ark Brought to the Temple[^A]
Then Solomon summoned to Jerusalem the elders of Israel, all the heads of the tribes and the chiefs of the Israelite families, to bring up the ark[^B] of the LORD's covenant from Zion, the City of David.
