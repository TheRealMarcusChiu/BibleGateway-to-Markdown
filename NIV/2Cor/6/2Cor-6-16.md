# 2 Corinthians 6:16 (New International Version)
What agreement is there between the temple of God and idols?[^A] For we are the temple[^B] of the living God.[^C] As God has said:
"I will live with them  
    and walk among them,  
and I will be their God,  
    and they will be my people."[^D]
