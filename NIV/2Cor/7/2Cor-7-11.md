# 2 Corinthians 7:11 (New International Version)
See what this godly sorrow has produced in you: what earnestness, what eagerness to clear yourselves, what indignation, what alarm, what longing, what concern,[^A] what readiness to see justice done. At every point you have proved yourselves to be innocent in this matter.
