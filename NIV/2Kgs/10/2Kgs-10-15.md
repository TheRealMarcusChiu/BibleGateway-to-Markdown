# 2 Kings 10:15 (New International Version)
After he left there, he came upon Jehonadab[^A] son of Rekab,[^B] who was on his way to meet him. Jehu greeted him and said, "Are you in accord with me, as I am with you?"
"I am," Jehonadab answered.
"If so," said Jehu, "give me your hand."[^C] So he did, and Jehu helped him up into the chariot.
