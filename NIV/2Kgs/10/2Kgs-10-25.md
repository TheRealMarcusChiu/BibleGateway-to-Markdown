# 2 Kings 10:25 (New International Version)
As soon as Jehu had finished making the burnt offering, he ordered the guards and officers: "Go in and kill[^A] them; let no one escape."[^B] So they cut them down with the sword. The guards and officers threw the bodies out and then entered the inner shrine of the temple of Baal.
