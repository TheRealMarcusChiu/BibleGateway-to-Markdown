# 2 Kings 11:18 (New International Version)
All the people of the land went to the temple[^A] of Baal and tore it down. They smashed[^B] the altars and idols to pieces and killed Mattan the priest[^C] of Baal in front of the altars.
Then Jehoiada the priest posted guards at the temple of the LORD.
