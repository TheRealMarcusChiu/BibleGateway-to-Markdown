# 2 Kings 11:4 (New International Version)
In the seventh year Jehoiada sent for the commanders of units of a hundred, the Carites[^A] and the guards and had them brought to him at the temple of the LORD. He made a covenant with them and put them under oath at the temple of the LORD. Then he showed them the king's son.
