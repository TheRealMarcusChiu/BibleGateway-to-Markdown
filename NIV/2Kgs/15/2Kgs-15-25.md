# 2 Kings 15:25 (New International Version)
One of his chief officers, Pekah[^A] son of Remaliah, conspired against him. Taking fifty men of Gilead with him, he assassinated[^B] Pekahiah, along with Argob and Arieh, in the citadel of the royal palace at Samaria. So Pekah killed Pekahiah and succeeded him as king.
