# 2 Kings 15:29 (New International Version)
In the time of Pekah king of Israel, Tiglath-Pileser[^A] king of Assyria came and took Ijon,[^B] Abel Beth Maakah, Janoah, Kedesh and Hazor. He took Gilead and Galilee, including all the land of Naphtali,[^C] and deported[^D] the people to Assyria.
