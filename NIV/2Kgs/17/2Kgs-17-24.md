# 2 Kings 17:24 (New International Version)
Samaria Resettled
The king of Assyria[^A] brought people from Babylon, Kuthah, Avva, Hamath and Sepharvaim[^B] and settled them in the towns of Samaria to replace the Israelites. They took over Samaria and lived in its towns.
