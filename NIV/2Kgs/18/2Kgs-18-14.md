# 2 Kings 18:14 (New International Version)
So Hezekiah king of Judah sent this message to the king of Assyria at Lachish:[^A] "I have done wrong.[^B] Withdraw from me, and I will pay whatever you demand of me." The king of Assyria exacted from Hezekiah king of Judah three hundred talents of silver and thirty talents of gold.
