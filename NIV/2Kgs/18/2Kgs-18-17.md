# 2 Kings 18:17 (New International Version)
Sennacherib Threatens Jerusalem[^A][^B]
The king of Assyria sent his supreme commander,[^C] his chief officer and his field commander with a large army, from Lachish to King Hezekiah at Jerusalem. They came up to Jerusalem and stopped at the aqueduct of the Upper Pool,[^D] on the road to the Washerman's Field.
