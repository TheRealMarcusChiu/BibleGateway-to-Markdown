# 2 Kings 2:16 (New International Version)
"Look," they said, "we your servants have fifty able men. Let them go and look for your master. Perhaps the Spirit[^A] of the LORD has picked him up[^B] and set him down on some mountain or in some valley."
"No," Elisha replied, "do not send them."
