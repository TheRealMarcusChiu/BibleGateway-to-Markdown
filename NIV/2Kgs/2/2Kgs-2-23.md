# 2 Kings 2:23 (New International Version)
Elisha Is Jeered
From there Elisha went up to Bethel. As he was walking along the road, some boys came out of the town and jeered[^A] at him. "Get out of here, baldy!" they said. "Get out of here, baldy!"
