# 2 Kings 22:13 (New International Version)
"Go and inquire[^A] of the LORD for me and for the people and for all Judah about what is written in this book that has been found. Great is the LORD's anger[^B] that burns against us because those who have gone before us have not obeyed the words of this book; they have not acted in accordance with all that is written there concerning us."
