# 2 Kings 22:14 (New International Version)
Hilkiah the priest, Ahikam, Akbor, Shaphan and Asaiah went to speak to the prophet[^A] Huldah, who was the wife of Shallum son of Tikvah, the son of Harhas, keeper of the wardrobe. She lived in Jerusalem, in the New Quarter.
