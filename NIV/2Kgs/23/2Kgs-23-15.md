# 2 Kings 23:15 (New International Version)
Even the altar[^A] at Bethel, the high place made by Jeroboam[^B] son of Nebat, who had caused Israel to sin--even that altar and high place he demolished. He burned the high place and ground it to powder, and burned the Asherah pole also.
