# 2 Kings 23:29 (New International Version)
While Josiah was king, Pharaoh Necho[^A] king of Egypt went up to the Euphrates River to help the king of Assyria. King Josiah marched out to meet him in battle, but Necho faced him and killed him at Megiddo.[^B]
