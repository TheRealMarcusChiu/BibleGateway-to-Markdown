# 2 Kings 23:36 (New International Version)
Jehoiakim King of Judah[^A]
Jehoiakim[^B] was twenty-five years old when he became king, and he reigned in Jerusalem eleven years. His mother's name was Zebidah daughter of Pedaiah; she was from Rumah.
