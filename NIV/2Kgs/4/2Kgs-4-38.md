# 2 Kings 4:38 (New International Version)
Death in the Pot
Elisha returned to Gilgal[^A] and there was a famine[^B] in that region. While the company of the prophets was meeting with him, he said to his servant, "Put on the large pot and cook some stew for these prophets."
