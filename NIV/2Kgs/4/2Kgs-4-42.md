# 2 Kings 4:42 (New International Version)
Feeding of a Hundred
A man came from Baal Shalishah,[^A] bringing the man of God twenty loaves[^B] of barley bread[^C] baked from the first ripe grain, along with some heads of new grain. "Give it to the people to eat," Elisha said.
