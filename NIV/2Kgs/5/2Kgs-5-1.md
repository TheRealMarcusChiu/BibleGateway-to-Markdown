# 2 Kings 5:1 (New International Version)
Naaman Healed of Leprosy
Now Naaman was commander of the army of the king of Aram.[^A] He was a great man in the sight of his master and highly regarded, because through him the LORD had given victory to Aram. He was a valiant soldier, but he had leprosy.[^B]
