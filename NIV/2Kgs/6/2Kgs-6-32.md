# 2 Kings 6:32 (New International Version)
Now Elisha was sitting in his house, and the elders[^A] were sitting with him. The king sent a messenger ahead, but before he arrived, Elisha said to the elders, "Don't you see how this murderer[^B] is sending someone to cut off my head?[^C] Look, when the messenger comes, shut the door and hold it shut against him. Is not the sound of his master's footsteps behind him?"
