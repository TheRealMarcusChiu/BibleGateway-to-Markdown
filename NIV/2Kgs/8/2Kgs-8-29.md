# 2 Kings 8:29 (New International Version)
so King Joram returned to Jezreel[^A] to recover from the wounds the Arameans had inflicted on him at Ramoth in his battle with Hazael[^B] king of Aram.
Then Ahaziah[^C] son of Jehoram king of Judah went down to Jezreel to see Joram son of Ahab, because he had been wounded.
