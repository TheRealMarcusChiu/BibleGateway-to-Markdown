# 2 Kings 9:17 (New International Version)
When the lookout[^A] standing on the tower in Jezreel saw Jehu's troops approaching, he called out, "I see some troops coming."
"Get a horseman," Joram ordered. "Send him to meet them and ask, 'Do you come in peace?[^B]'"
