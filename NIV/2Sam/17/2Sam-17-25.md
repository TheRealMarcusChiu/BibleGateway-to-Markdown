# 2 Samuel 17:25 (New International Version)
Absalom had appointed Amasa[^A] over the army in place of Joab. Amasa was the son of Jether,[^B] an Ishmaelite who had married Abigail, the daughter of Nahash and sister of Zeruiah the mother of Joab.
