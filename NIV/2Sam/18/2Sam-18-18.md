# 2 Samuel 18:18 (New International Version)
During his lifetime Absalom had taken a pillar and erected it in the King's Valley[^A] as a monument[^B] to himself, for he thought, "I have no son[^C] to carry on the memory of my name." He named the pillar after himself, and it is called Absalom's Monument to this day.
