# 2 Samuel 23:20 (New International Version)
Benaiah[^A] son of Jehoiada, a valiant fighter from Kabzeel,[^B] performed great exploits. He struck down Moab's two mightiest warriors. He also went down into a pit on a snowy day and killed a lion.
