# 2 Samuel 24:16 (New International Version)
When the angel stretched out his hand to destroy Jerusalem, the LORD relented[^A] concerning the disaster and said to the angel who was afflicting the people, "Enough! Withdraw your hand." The angel of the LORD[^B] was then at the threshing floor of Araunah the Jebusite.
