# 2 Samuel 5:8 (New International Version)
On that day David had said, "Anyone who conquers the Jebusites will have to use the water shaft[^A] to reach those 'lame and blind'[^B] who are David's enemies." That is why they say, "The 'blind and lame' will not enter the palace."
