# Acts 18:18 (New International Version)
Priscilla, Aquila and Apollos
Paul stayed on in Corinth for some time. Then he left the brothers and sisters[^A] and sailed for Syria,[^B] accompanied by Priscilla and Aquila.[^C] Before he sailed, he had his hair cut off at Cenchreae[^D] because of a vow he had taken.[^E]
