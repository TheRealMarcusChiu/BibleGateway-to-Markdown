# Amos 2:4 (New International Version)
This is what the LORD says:
"For three sins of Judah,[^A]  
    even for four, I will not relent.  
Because they have rejected the law[^B] of the LORD  
    and have not kept his decrees,[^C]  
because they have been led astray[^D] by false gods,[^E]  
    the gods their ancestors followed,[^F]
