# Amos 4:11 (New International Version)
"I overthrew some of you  
    as I overthrew Sodom and Gomorrah.[^A]  
You were like a burning stick[^B] snatched from the fire,  
    yet you have not returned to me,"  
declares the LORD.[^C]
