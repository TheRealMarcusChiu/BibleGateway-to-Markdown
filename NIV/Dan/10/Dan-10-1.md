# Daniel 10:1 (New International Version)
Daniel's Vision of a Man
In the third year of Cyrus[^A] king of Persia, a revelation was given to Daniel (who was called Belteshazzar).[^B] Its message was true[^C] and it concerned a great war. The understanding of the message came to him in a vision.
