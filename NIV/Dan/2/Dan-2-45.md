# Daniel 2:45 (New International Version)
This is the meaning of the vision of the rock[^A] cut out of a mountain, but not by human hands[^B]--a rock that broke the iron, the bronze, the clay, the silver and the gold to pieces.
"The great God has shown the king what will take place in the future.[^C] The dream is true[^D] and its interpretation is trustworthy."
