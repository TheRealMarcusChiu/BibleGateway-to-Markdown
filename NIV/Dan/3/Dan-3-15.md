# Daniel 3:15 (New International Version)
Now when you hear the sound of the horn, flute, zither, lyre, harp, pipe and all kinds of music, if you are ready to fall down and worship the image I made, very good. But if you do not worship it, you will be thrown immediately into a blazing furnace. Then what god[^A] will be able to rescue[^B] you from my hand?"
