# Daniel 4:19 (New International Version)
Daniel Interprets the Dream
Then Daniel (also called Belteshazzar) was greatly perplexed for a time, and his thoughts terrified[^A] him. So the king said, "Belteshazzar, do not let the dream or its meaning alarm you."[^B]
Belteshazzar answered, "My lord, if only the dream applied to your enemies and its meaning to your adversaries!
