# Daniel 4:25 (New International Version)
You will be driven away from people and will live with the wild animals; you will eat grass like the ox and be drenched[^A] with the dew of heaven. Seven times will pass by for you until you acknowledge that the Most High[^B] is sovereign over all kingdoms on earth and gives them to anyone he wishes.[^C]
