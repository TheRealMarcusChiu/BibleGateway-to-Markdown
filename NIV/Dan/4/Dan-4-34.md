# Daniel 4:34 (New International Version)
At the end of that time, I, Nebuchadnezzar, raised my eyes toward heaven, and my sanity[^A] was restored. Then I praised the Most High; I honored and glorified him who lives forever.[^B]
His dominion is an eternal dominion;  
    his kingdom[^C] endures from generation to generation.[^D]
