# Daniel 5:23 (New International Version)
Instead, you have set yourself up against[^A] the Lord of heaven. You had the goblets from his temple brought to you, and you and your nobles, your wives[^B] and your concubines drank wine from them. You praised the gods of silver and gold, of bronze, iron, wood and stone, which cannot see or hear or understand.[^C] But you did not honor the God who holds in his hand your life[^D] and all your ways.[^E]
