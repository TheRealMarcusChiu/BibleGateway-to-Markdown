# Daniel 6:24 (New International Version)
At the king's command, the men who had falsely accused Daniel were brought in and thrown into the lions' den,[^A] along with their wives and children.[^B] And before they reached the floor of the den, the lions overpowered them and crushed all their bones.[^C]
