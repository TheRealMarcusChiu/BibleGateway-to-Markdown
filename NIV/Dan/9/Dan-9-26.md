# Daniel 9:26 (New International Version)
After the sixty-two 'sevens,' the Anointed One will be put to death[^A] and will have nothing. The people of the ruler who will come will destroy the city and the sanctuary. The end will come like a flood:[^B] War will continue until the end, and desolations[^C] have been decreed.[^D]
