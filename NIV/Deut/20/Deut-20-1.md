# Deuteronomy 20:1 (New International Version)
Going to War
When you go to war against your enemies and see horses and chariots and an army greater than yours,[^A] do not be afraid[^B] of them,[^C] because the LORD your God, who brought you up out of Egypt, will be with[^D] you.
