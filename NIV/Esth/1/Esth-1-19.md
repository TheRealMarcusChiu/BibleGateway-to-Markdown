# Esther 1:19 (New International Version)
"Therefore, if it pleases the king,[^A] let him issue a royal decree and let it be written in the laws of Persia and Media, which cannot be repealed,[^B] that Vashti is never again to enter the presence of King Xerxes. Also let the king give her royal position to someone else who is better than she.
