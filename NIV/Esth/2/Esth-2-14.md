# Esther 2:14 (New International Version)
In the evening she would go there and in the morning return to another part of the harem to the care of Shaashgaz, the king's eunuch who was in charge of the concubines.[^A] She would not return to the king unless he was pleased with her and summoned her by name.[^B]
