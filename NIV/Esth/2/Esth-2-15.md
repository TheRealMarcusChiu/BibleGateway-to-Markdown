# Esther 2:15 (New International Version)
When the turn came for Esther (the young woman Mordecai had adopted, the daughter of his uncle Abihail[^A]) to go to the king,[^B] she asked for nothing other than what Hegai, the king's eunuch who was in charge of the harem, suggested. And Esther won the favor[^C] of everyone who saw her.
