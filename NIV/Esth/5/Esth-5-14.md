# Esther 5:14 (New International Version)
His wife Zeresh and all his friends said to him, "Have a pole set up, reaching to a height of fifty cubits,[^A] and ask the king in the morning to have Mordecai impaled[^B] on it. Then go with the king to the banquet and enjoy yourself." This suggestion delighted Haman, and he had the pole set up.
