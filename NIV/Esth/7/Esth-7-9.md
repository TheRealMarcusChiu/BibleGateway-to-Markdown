# Esther 7:9 (New International Version)
Then Harbona,[^A] one of the eunuchs attending the king, said, "A pole reaching to a height of fifty cubits[^B] stands by Haman's house. He had it set up for Mordecai, who spoke up to help the king."
The king said, "Impale him on it!"[^C]
