# Exodus 22:9 (New International Version)
In all cases of illegal possession of an ox, a donkey, a sheep, a garment, or any other lost property about which somebody says, 'This is mine,' both parties are to bring their cases before the judges.[^A] The one whom the judges declare guilty must pay back double to the other.
