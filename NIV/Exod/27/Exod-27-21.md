# Exodus 27:21 (New International Version)
In the tent of meeting,[^A] outside the curtain that shields the ark of the covenant law,[^B] Aaron and his sons are to keep the lamps[^C] burning before the LORD from evening till morning. This is to be a lasting ordinance[^D] among the Israelites for the generations to come.
