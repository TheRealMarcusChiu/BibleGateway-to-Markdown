# Exodus 28:30 (New International Version)
Also put the Urim and the Thummim[^A] in the breastpiece, so they may be over Aaron's heart whenever he enters the presence of the LORD. Thus Aaron will always bear the means of making decisions for the Israelites over his heart before the LORD.
