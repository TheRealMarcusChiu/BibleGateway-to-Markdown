# Exodus 3:15 (New International Version)
God also said to Moses, "Say to the Israelites, 'The LORD, the God of your fathers[^A]--the God of Abraham, the God of Isaac and the God of Jacob[^B]--has sent me to you.'
"This is my name[^C] forever,  
    the name you shall call me  
    from generation to generation.[^D]
