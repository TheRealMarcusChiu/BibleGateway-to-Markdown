# Ezekiel 16:52 (New International Version)
Bear your disgrace, for you have furnished some justification for your sisters. Because your sins were more vile than theirs, they appear more righteous[^A] than you. So then, be ashamed and bear[^B] your disgrace, for you have made your sisters appear righteous.
