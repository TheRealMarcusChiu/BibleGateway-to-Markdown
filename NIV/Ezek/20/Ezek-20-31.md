# Ezekiel 20:31 (New International Version)
When you offer your gifts--the sacrifice of your children[^A] in the fire--you continue to defile yourselves with all your idols to this day. Am I to let you inquire of me, you Israelites? As surely as I live, declares the Sovereign LORD, I will not let you inquire of me.[^B]
