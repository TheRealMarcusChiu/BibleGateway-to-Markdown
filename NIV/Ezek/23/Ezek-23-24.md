# Ezekiel 23:24 (New International Version)
They will come against you with weapons, chariots and wagons[^A] and with a throng of people; they will take up positions against you on every side with large and small shields and with helmets. I will turn you over to them for punishment,[^B] and they will punish you according to their standards.
