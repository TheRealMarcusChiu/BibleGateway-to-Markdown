# Ezekiel 31:14 (New International Version)
Therefore no other trees by the waters are ever to tower proudly on high, lifting their tops above the thick foliage. No other trees so well-watered are ever to reach such a height; they are all destined[^A] for death,[^B] for the earth below, among mortals who go down to the realm of the dead.[^C]
