# Ezekiel 31:8 (New International Version)
The cedars[^A] in the garden of God  
    could not rival it,  
nor could the junipers  
    equal its boughs,  
nor could the plane trees[^B]  
    compare with its branches--  
no tree in the garden of God  
    could match its beauty.[^C]
