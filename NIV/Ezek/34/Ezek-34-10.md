# Ezekiel 34:10 (New International Version)
This is what the Sovereign LORD says: I am against[^A] the shepherds and will hold them accountable for my flock. I will remove them from tending the flock so that the shepherds can no longer feed themselves. I will rescue[^B] my flock from their mouths, and it will no longer be food for them.[^C]
