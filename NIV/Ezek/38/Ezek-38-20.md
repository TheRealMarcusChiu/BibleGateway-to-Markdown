# Ezekiel 38:20 (New International Version)
The fish in the sea, the birds in the sky, the beasts of the field, every creature that moves along the ground, and all the people on the face of the earth will tremble[^A] at my presence. The mountains will be overturned,[^B] the cliffs will crumble[^C] and every wall will fall to the ground.[^D]
