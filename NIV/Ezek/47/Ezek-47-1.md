# Ezekiel 47:1 (New International Version)
The River From the Temple
The man brought me back to the entrance to the temple, and I saw water[^A] coming out from under the threshold of the temple toward the east (for the temple faced east). The water was coming down from under the south side of the temple, south of the altar.[^B]
