# Ezekiel 47:22 (New International Version)
You are to allot it as an inheritance[^A] for yourselves and for the foreigners[^B] residing among you and who have children. You are to consider them as native-born Israelites; along with you they are to be allotted an inheritance among the tribes of Israel.[^C]
