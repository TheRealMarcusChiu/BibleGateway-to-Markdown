# Ezekiel 8:16 (New International Version)
He then brought me into the inner court[^A] of the house of the LORD, and there at the entrance to the temple, between the portico and the altar,[^B] were about twenty-five men. With their backs toward the temple of the LORD and their faces toward the east, they were bowing down to the sun[^C] in the east.[^D]
