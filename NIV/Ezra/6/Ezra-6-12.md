# Ezra 6:12 (New International Version)
May God, who has caused his Name to dwell there,[^A] overthrow any king or people who lifts a hand to change this decree or to destroy this temple in Jerusalem.
I Darius[^B] have decreed it. Let it be carried out with diligence.
