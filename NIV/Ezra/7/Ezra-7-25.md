# Ezra 7:25 (New International Version)
And you, Ezra, in accordance with the wisdom of your God, which you possess, appoint[^A] magistrates and judges to administer justice to all the people of Trans-Euphrates--all who know the laws of your God. And you are to teach[^B] any who do not know them.
