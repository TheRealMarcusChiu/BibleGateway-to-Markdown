# Genesis 3:17 (New International Version)
To Adam he said, "Because you listened to your wife and ate fruit from the tree about which I commanded you, 'You must not eat from it,'[^A]
"Cursed[^B] is the ground[^C] because of you;  
    through painful toil[^D] you will eat food from it  
    all the days of your life.[^E]
