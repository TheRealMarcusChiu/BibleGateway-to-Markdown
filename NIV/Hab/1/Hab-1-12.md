# Habakkuk 1:12 (New International Version)
Habakkuk's Second Complaint
LORD, are you not from everlasting?[^A]  
    My God, my Holy One,[^B] you will never die.[^C]  
You, LORD, have appointed[^D] them to execute judgment;  
    you, my Rock,[^E] have ordained them to punish.
