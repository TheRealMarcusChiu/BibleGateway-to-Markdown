# Habakkuk 2:16 (New International Version)
You will be filled with shame[^A] instead of glory.[^B]  
    Now it is your turn! Drink[^C] and let your nakedness be exposed![^D]  
The cup[^E] from the LORD's right hand is coming around to you,  
    and disgrace will cover your glory.
