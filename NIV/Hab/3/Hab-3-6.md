# Habakkuk 3:6 (New International Version)
He stood, and shook the earth;  
    he looked, and made the nations tremble.  
The ancient mountains crumbled[^A]  
    and the age-old hills[^B] collapsed[^C]--  
    but he marches on forever.[^D]
