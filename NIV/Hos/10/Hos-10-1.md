# Hosea 10:1 (New International Version)
Israel was a spreading vine;[^A]  
    he brought forth fruit for himself.  
As his fruit increased,  
    he built more altars;[^B]  
as his land prospered,[^C]  
    he adorned his sacred stones.[^D]
