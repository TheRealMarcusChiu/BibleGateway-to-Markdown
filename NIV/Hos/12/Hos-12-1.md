# Hosea 12:1 (New International Version)
Ephraim[^A] feeds on the wind;[^B]  
    he pursues the east wind all day  
    and multiplies lies and violence.[^C]  
He makes a treaty with Assyria[^D]  
    and sends olive oil to Egypt.[^E]
