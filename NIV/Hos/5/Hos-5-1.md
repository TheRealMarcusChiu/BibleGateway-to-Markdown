# Hosea 5:1 (New International Version)
Judgment Against Israel
"Hear this, you priests!  
    Pay attention, you Israelites!  
Listen, royal house!  
    This judgment[^A] is against you:  
You have been a snare[^B] at Mizpah,  
    a net[^C] spread out on Tabor.
