# Isaiah 1:18 (New International Version)
"Come now, let us settle the matter,"[^A]  
    says the LORD.  
"Though your sins are like scarlet,  
    they shall be as white as snow;[^B]  
though they are red as crimson,  
    they shall be like wool.[^C]
