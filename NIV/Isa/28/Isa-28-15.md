# Isaiah 28:15 (New International Version)
You boast, "We have entered into a covenant with death,[^A]  
    with the realm of the dead we have made an agreement.  
When an overwhelming scourge sweeps by,[^B]  
    it cannot touch us,  
for we have made a lie[^C] our refuge  
    and falsehood our hiding place.[^D]"
