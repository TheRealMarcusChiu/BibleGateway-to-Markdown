# Isaiah 29:13 (New International Version)
The Lord says:
"These people[^A] come near to me with their mouth  
    and honor me with their lips,[^B]  
    but their hearts are far from me.[^C]  
Their worship of me  
    is based on merely human rules they have been taught.[^D]
