# Isaiah 29:16 (New International Version)
You turn things upside down,  
    as if the potter were thought to be like the clay![^A]  
Shall what is formed say to the one who formed[^B] it,  
    "You did not make me"?  
Can the pot say to the potter,[^C]  
    "You know nothing"?[^D]
