# Isaiah 30:10 (New International Version)
They say to the seers,[^A]  
    "See no more visions[^B]!"  
and to the prophets,  
    "Give us no more visions of what is right!  
Tell us pleasant things,[^C]  
    prophesy illusions.[^D]
