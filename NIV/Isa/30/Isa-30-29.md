# Isaiah 30:29 (New International Version)
And you will sing  
    as on the night you celebrate a holy festival;[^A]  
your hearts will rejoice[^B]  
    as when people playing pipes[^C] go up  
to the mountain[^D] of the LORD,  
    to the Rock[^E] of Israel.
