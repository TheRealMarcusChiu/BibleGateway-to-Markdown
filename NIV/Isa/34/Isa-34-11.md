# Isaiah 34:11 (New International Version)
The desert owl[^A] and screech owl will possess it;  
    the great owl and the raven[^B] will nest there.  
God will stretch out over Edom[^C]  
    the measuring line of chaos[^D]  
    and the plumb line[^E] of desolation.
