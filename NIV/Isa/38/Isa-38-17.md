# Isaiah 38:17 (New International Version)
Surely it was for my benefit[^A]  
    that I suffered such anguish.[^B]  
In your love you kept me  
    from the pit[^C] of destruction;  
you have put all my sins[^D]  
    behind your back.[^E]
