# Isaiah 45:19 (New International Version)
I have not spoken in secret,[^A]  
    from somewhere in a land of darkness;[^B]  
I have not said to Jacob's descendants,[^C]  
    'Seek[^D] me in vain.'  
I, the LORD, speak the truth;  
    I declare what is right.[^E]
