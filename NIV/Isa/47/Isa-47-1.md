# Isaiah 47:1 (New International Version)
The Fall of Babylon
"Go down, sit in the dust,[^A]  
    Virgin Daughter[^B] Babylon;  
sit on the ground without a throne,  
    queen city of the Babylonians.[^C]  
No more will you be called  
    tender or delicate.[^D]
