# Isaiah 47:6 (New International Version)
I was angry[^A] with my people  
    and desecrated my inheritance;[^B]  
I gave them into your hand,[^C]  
    and you showed them no mercy.[^D]  
Even on the aged  
    you laid a very heavy yoke.
