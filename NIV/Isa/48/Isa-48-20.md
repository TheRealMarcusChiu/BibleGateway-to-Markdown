# Isaiah 48:20 (New International Version)
Leave Babylon,  
    flee[^A] from the Babylonians!  
Announce this with shouts of joy[^B]  
    and proclaim it.  
Send it out to the ends of the earth;[^C]  
    say, "The LORD has redeemed[^D] his servant Jacob."
