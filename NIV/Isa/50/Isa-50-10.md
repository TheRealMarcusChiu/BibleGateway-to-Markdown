# Isaiah 50:10 (New International Version)
Who among you fears[^A] the LORD  
    and obeys[^B] the word of his servant?[^C]  
Let the one who walks in the dark,  
    who has no light,[^D]  
trust[^E] in the name of the LORD  
    and rely on their God.
