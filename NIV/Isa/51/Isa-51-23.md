# Isaiah 51:23 (New International Version)
I will put it into the hands of your tormentors,[^A]  
    who said to you,  
    'Fall prostrate[^B] that we may walk[^C] on you.'  
And you made your back like the ground,  
    like a street to be walked on."[^D]
