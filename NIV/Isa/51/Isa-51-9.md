Awake, awake,[^A] arm[^B] of the LORD,  
    clothe yourself with strength![^C]  
Awake, as in days gone by,  
    as in generations of old.[^D]  
Was it not you who cut Rahab[^E] to pieces,  
    who pierced that monster[^F] through?
