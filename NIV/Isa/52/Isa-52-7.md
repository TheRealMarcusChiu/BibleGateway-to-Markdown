# Isaiah 52:7 (New International Version)
How beautiful on the mountains[^A]  
    are the feet of those who bring good news,[^B]  
who proclaim peace,[^C]  
    who bring good tidings,  
    who proclaim salvation,  
who say to Zion,  
    "Your God reigns!"[^D]
