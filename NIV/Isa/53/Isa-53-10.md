# Isaiah 53:10 (New International Version)
Yet it was the LORD's will[^A] to crush[^B] him and cause him to suffer,[^C]  
    and though the LORD makes his life an offering for sin,[^D]  
he will see his offspring[^E] and prolong his days,  
    and the will of the LORD will prosper[^F] in his hand.
