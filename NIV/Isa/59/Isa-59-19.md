# Isaiah 59:19 (New International Version)
From the west,[^A] people will fear the name of the LORD,  
    and from the rising of the sun,[^B] they will revere his glory.[^C]  
For he will come like a pent-up flood  
    that the breath[^D] of the LORD drives along.
