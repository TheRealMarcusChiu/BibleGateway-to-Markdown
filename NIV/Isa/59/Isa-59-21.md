# Isaiah 59:21 (New International Version)
"As for me, this is my covenant[^A] with them," says the LORD. "My Spirit,[^B] who is on you, will not depart from you,[^C] and my words that I have put in your mouth[^D] will always be on your lips, on the lips of your children and on the lips of their descendants--from this time on and forever," says the LORD.
