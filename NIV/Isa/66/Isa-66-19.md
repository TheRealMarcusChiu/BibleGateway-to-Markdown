# Isaiah 66:19 (New International Version)
"I will set a sign[^A] among them, and I will send some of those who survive[^B] to the nations--to Tarshish,[^C] to the Libyans and Lydians[^D] (famous as archers), to Tubal[^E] and Greece,[^F] and to the distant islands[^G] that have not heard of my fame or seen my glory.[^H] They will proclaim my glory among the nations.
