# Jeremiah 10:5 (New International Version)
Like a scarecrow in a cucumber field,  
    their idols cannot speak;[^A]  
they must be carried  
    because they cannot walk.[^B]  
Do not fear them;  
    they can do no harm[^C]  
    nor can they do any good."[^D]
