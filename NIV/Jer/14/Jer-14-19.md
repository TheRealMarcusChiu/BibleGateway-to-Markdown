# Jeremiah 14:19 (New International Version)
Have you rejected Judah completely?[^A]  
    Do you despise Zion?  
Why have you afflicted us  
    so that we cannot be healed?[^B]  
We hoped for peace  
    but no good has come,  
for a time of healing  
    but there is only terror.[^C]
