# Jeremiah 2:8 (New International Version)
The priests did not ask,  
    'Where is the LORD?'  
Those who deal with the law did not know me;[^A]  
    the leaders[^B] rebelled against me.  
The prophets prophesied by Baal,[^C]  
    following worthless idols.[^D]
