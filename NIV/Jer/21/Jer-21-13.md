# Jeremiah 21:13 (New International Version)
I am against[^A] you, Jerusalem,  
    you who live above this valley[^B]  
    on the rocky plateau, declares the LORD--  
you who say, "Who can come against us?  
    Who can enter our refuge?"[^C]
