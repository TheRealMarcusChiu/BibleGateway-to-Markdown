# Jeremiah 23:10 (New International Version)
The land is full of adulterers;[^A]  
    because of the curse[^B] the land lies parched  
    and the pastures[^C] in the wilderness are withered.[^D]  
The prophets follow an evil course  
    and use their power unjustly.
