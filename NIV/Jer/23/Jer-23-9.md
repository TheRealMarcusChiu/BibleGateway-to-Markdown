# Jeremiah 23:9 (New International Version)
Lying Prophets
Concerning the prophets:
My heart[^A] is broken within me;  
    all my bones tremble.[^B]  
I am like a drunken man,  
    like a strong man overcome by wine,  
because of the LORD  
    and his holy words.[^C]
