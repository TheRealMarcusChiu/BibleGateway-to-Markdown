# Jeremiah 25:1 (New International Version)
Seventy Years of Captivity
The word came to Jeremiah concerning all the people of Judah in the fourth year of Jehoiakim[^A] son of Josiah king of Judah, which was the first year of Nebuchadnezzar[^B] king of Babylon.
