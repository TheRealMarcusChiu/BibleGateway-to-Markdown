I will summon[^A] all the peoples of the north[^B] and my servant[^C] Nebuchadnezzar[^D] king of Babylon," declares the LORD, "and I will bring them against this land and its inhabitants and against all the surrounding nations. I will completely destroy[^E] them and make them an object of horror and scorn,[^F] and an everlasting ruin.[^G]
