# Jeremiah 26:18 (New International Version)
"Micah[^A] of Moresheth prophesied in the days of Hezekiah king of Judah. He told all the people of Judah, 'This is what the LORD Almighty says:
"'Zion[^B] will be plowed like a field,  
    Jerusalem will become a heap of rubble,[^C]  
    the temple hill[^D] a mound overgrown with thickets.'[^E]
