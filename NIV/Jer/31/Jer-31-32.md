# Jeremiah 31:32 (New International Version)
It will not be like the covenant[^A]  
    I made with their ancestors[^B]  
when I took them by the hand  
    to lead them out of Egypt,[^C]  
because they broke my covenant,  
    though I was a husband[^D] to them,"  
declares the LORD.
