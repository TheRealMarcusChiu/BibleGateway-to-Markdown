# Jeremiah 31:35 (New International Version)
This is what the LORD says,
he who appoints[^A] the sun  
    to shine by day,  
who decrees the moon and stars  
    to shine by night,[^B]  
who stirs up the sea[^C]  
    so that its waves roar[^D]--  
    the LORD Almighty is his name:[^E]
