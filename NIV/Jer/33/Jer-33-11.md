# Jeremiah 33:11 (New International Version)
the sounds of joy and gladness,[^A] the voices of bride and bridegroom, and the voices of those who bring thank offerings[^B] to the house of the LORD, saying,
"Give thanks to the LORD Almighty,  
    for the LORD is good;[^C]  
    his love endures forever."[^D]
For I will restore the fortunes[^E] of the land as they were before,[^F]' says the LORD.
