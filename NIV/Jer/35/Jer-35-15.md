# Jeremiah 35:15 (New International Version)
Again and again I sent all my servants the prophets[^A] to you. They said, "Each of you must turn[^B] from your wicked ways and reform[^C] your actions; do not follow other gods[^D] to serve them. Then you will live in the land[^E] I have given to you and your ancestors." But you have not paid attention or listened[^F] to me.
