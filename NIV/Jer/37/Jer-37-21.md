# Jeremiah 37:21 (New International Version)
King Zedekiah then gave orders for Jeremiah to be placed in the courtyard of the guard and given a loaf of bread from the street of the bakers each day until all the bread[^A] in the city was gone.[^B] So Jeremiah remained in the courtyard of the guard.[^C]
