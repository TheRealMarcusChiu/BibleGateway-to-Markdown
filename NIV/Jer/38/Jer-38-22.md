# Jeremiah 38:22 (New International Version)
All the women[^A] left in the palace of the king of Judah will be brought out to the officials of the king of Babylon. Those women will say to you:
"'They misled you and overcame you--  
    those trusted friends[^B] of yours.  
Your feet are sunk in the mud;[^C]  
    your friends have deserted you.'
