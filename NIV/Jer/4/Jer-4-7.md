# Jeremiah 4:7 (New International Version)
A lion[^A] has come out of his lair;[^B]  
    a destroyer[^C] of nations has set out.  
He has left his place  
    to lay waste[^D] your land.  
Your towns will lie in ruins[^E]  
    without inhabitant.
