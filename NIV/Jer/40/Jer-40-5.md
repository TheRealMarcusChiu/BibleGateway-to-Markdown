# Jeremiah 40:5 (New International Version)
However, before Jeremiah turned to go, Nebuzaradan added, "Go back to Gedaliah[^A] son of Ahikam,[^B] the son of Shaphan, whom the king of Babylon has appointed[^C] over the towns[^D] of Judah, and live with him among the people, or go anywhere else you please."[^E]
Then the commander gave him provisions and a present[^F] and let him go.
