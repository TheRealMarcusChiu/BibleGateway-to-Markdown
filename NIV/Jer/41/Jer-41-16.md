# Jeremiah 41:16 (New International Version)
Flight to Egypt
Then Johanan son of Kareah and all the army officers[^A] who were with him led away all the people of Mizpah who had survived,[^B] whom Johanan had recovered from Ishmael son of Nethaniah after Ishmael had assassinated Gedaliah son of Ahikam--the soldiers, women, children and court officials he had recovered from Gibeon.
