# Jeremiah 42:18 (New International Version)
This is what the LORD Almighty, the God of Israel, says: 'As my anger and wrath[^A] have been poured out on those who lived in Jerusalem,[^B] so will my wrath be poured out on you when you go to Egypt. You will be a curse[^C] and an object of horror,[^D] a curse and an object of reproach;[^E] you will never see this place again.'[^F]
