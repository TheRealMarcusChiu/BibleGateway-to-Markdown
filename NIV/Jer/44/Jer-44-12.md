# Jeremiah 44:12 (New International Version)
I will take away the remnant[^A] of Judah who were determined to go to Egypt to settle there. They will all perish in Egypt; they will fall by the sword or die from famine. From the least to the greatest,[^B] they will die by sword or famine.[^C] They will become a curse and an object of horror, a curse and an object of reproach.[^D]
