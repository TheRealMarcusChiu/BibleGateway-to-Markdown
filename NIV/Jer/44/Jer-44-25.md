# Jeremiah 44:25 (New International Version)
This is what the LORD Almighty, the God of Israel, says: You and your wives[^A] have done what you said you would do when you promised, 'We will certainly carry out the vows we made to burn incense and pour out drink offerings to the Queen of Heaven.'[^B]
"Go ahead then, do what you promised! Keep your vows![^C]
