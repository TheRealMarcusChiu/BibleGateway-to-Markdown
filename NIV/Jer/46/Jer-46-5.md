# Jeremiah 46:5 (New International Version)
What do I see?  
    They are terrified,  
they are retreating,  
    their warriors are defeated.  
They flee[^A] in haste  
    without looking back,  
    and there is terror[^B] on every side,"  
declares the LORD.
