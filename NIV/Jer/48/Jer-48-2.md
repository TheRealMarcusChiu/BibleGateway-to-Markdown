# Jeremiah 48:2 (New International Version)
Moab will be praised[^A] no more;  
    in Heshbon[^B] people will plot her downfall:  
    'Come, let us put an end to that nation.'[^C]  
You, the people of Madmen, will also be silenced;  
    the sword will pursue you.
