# Jeremiah 48:44 (New International Version)
"Whoever flees[^A] from the terror  
    will fall into a pit,  
whoever climbs out of the pit  
    will be caught in a snare;  
for I will bring on Moab  
    the year[^B] of her punishment,"  
declares the LORD.
