# Jeremiah 49:1 (New International Version)
A Message About Ammon
Concerning the Ammonites:[^A]
This is what the LORD says:
"Has Israel no sons?  
    Has Israel no heir?  
Why then has Molek[^B] taken possession of Gad?[^C]  
    Why do his people live in its towns?
