# Jeremiah 49:28 (New International Version)
A Message About Kedar and Hazor
Concerning Kedar[^A] and the kingdoms of Hazor,[^B] which Nebuchadnezzar[^C] king of Babylon attacked:
This is what the LORD says:
"Arise, and attack Kedar  
    and destroy the people of the East.[^D]
