# Jeremiah 49:32 (New International Version)
Their camels[^A] will become plunder,  
    and their large herds[^B] will be spoils of war.  
I will scatter to the winds[^C] those who are in distant places[^D]  
    and will bring disaster on them from every side,"  
declares the LORD.
