# Jeremiah 9:3 (New International Version)
"They make ready their tongue  
    like a bow, to shoot lies;[^A]  
it is not by truth  
    that they triumph in the land.  
They go from one sin to another;  
    they do not acknowledge[^B] me,"  
declares the LORD.
