# Joel 1:12 (New International Version)
The vine is dried up  
    and the fig tree is withered;[^A]  
the pomegranate,[^B] the palm and the apple tree--  
    all the trees of the field--are dried up.[^C]  
Surely the people's joy  
    is withered away.
