# Joel 2:11 (New International Version)
The LORD[^A] thunders[^B]  
    at the head of his army;[^C]  
his forces are beyond number,  
    and mighty is the army that obeys his command.  
The day of the LORD is great;[^D]  
    it is dreadful.  
    Who can endure it?[^E]
