# Joshua 10:12 (New International Version)
On the day the LORD gave the Amorites[^A] over to Israel, Joshua said to the LORD in the presence of Israel:
"Sun, stand still over Gibeon,  
    and you, moon, over the Valley of Aijalon.[^B]"
