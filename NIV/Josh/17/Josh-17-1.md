# Joshua 17:1 (New International Version)
This was the allotment for the tribe of Manasseh[^A] as Joseph's firstborn,[^B] that is, for Makir,[^C] Manasseh's firstborn. Makir was the ancestor of the Gileadites, who had received Gilead[^D] and Bashan[^E] because the Makirites were great soldiers.
