# Joshua 22:19 (New International Version)
If the land you possess is defiled, come over to the LORD's land, where the LORD's tabernacle[^A] stands, and share the land with us. But do not rebel against the LORD or against us by building an altar[^B] for yourselves, other than the altar of the LORD our God.
