# Joshua 22:27 (New International Version)
On the contrary, it is to be a witness[^A] between us and you and the generations that follow, that we will worship the LORD at his sanctuary with our burnt offerings, sacrifices and fellowship offerings.[^B] Then in the future your descendants will not be able to say to ours, 'You have no share in the LORD.'
