# Joshua 6:26 (New International Version)
At that time Joshua pronounced this solemn oath:[^A] "Cursed[^B] before the LORD is the one who undertakes to rebuild this city, Jericho:
"At the cost of his firstborn son  
    he will lay its foundations;  
at the cost of his youngest  
    he will set up its gates."[^C]
