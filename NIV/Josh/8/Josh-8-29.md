# Joshua 8:29 (New International Version)
He impaled the body of the king of Ai on a pole and left it there until evening. At sunset,[^A] Joshua ordered them to take the body from the pole and throw it down at the entrance of the city gate. And they raised a large pile of rocks[^B] over it, which remains to this day.
