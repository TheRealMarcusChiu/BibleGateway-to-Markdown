# Jude 12 (New International Version)
These people are blemishes at your love feasts,[^A] eating with you without the slightest qualm--shepherds who feed only themselves.[^B] They are clouds without rain,[^C] blown along by the wind;[^D] autumn trees, without fruit and uprooted[^E]--twice dead.
