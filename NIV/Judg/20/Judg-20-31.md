# Judges 20:31 (New International Version)
The Benjamites came out to meet them and were drawn away[^A] from the city. They began to inflict casualties on the Israelites as before, so that about thirty men fell in the open field and on the roads--the one leading to Bethel[^B] and the other to Gibeah.
