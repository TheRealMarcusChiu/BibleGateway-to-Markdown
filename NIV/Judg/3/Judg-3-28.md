# Judges 3:28 (New International Version)
"Follow me," he ordered, "for the LORD has given Moab,[^A] your enemy, into your hands.[^B]" So they followed him down and took possession of the fords of the Jordan[^C] that led to Moab; they allowed no one to cross over.
