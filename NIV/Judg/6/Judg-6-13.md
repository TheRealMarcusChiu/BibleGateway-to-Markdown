# Judges 6:13 (New International Version)
"Pardon me, my lord," Gideon replied, "but if the LORD is with us, why has all this happened to us? Where are all his wonders[^A] that our ancestors told[^B] us about when they said, 'Did not the LORD bring us up out of Egypt?' But now the LORD has abandoned[^C] us and given us into the hand of Midian."
