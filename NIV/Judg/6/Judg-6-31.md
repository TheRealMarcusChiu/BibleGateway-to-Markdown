# Judges 6:31 (New International Version)
But Joash replied to the hostile crowd around him, "Are you going to plead Baal's cause?[^A] Are you trying to save him? Whoever fights for him shall be put to death by morning! If Baal really is a god, he can defend himself when someone breaks down his altar."
