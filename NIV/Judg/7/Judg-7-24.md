# Judges 7:24 (New International Version)
Gideon sent messengers throughout the hill country of Ephraim, saying, "Come down against the Midianites and seize the waters of the Jordan[^A] ahead of them as far as Beth Barah."
So all the men of Ephraim were called out and they seized the waters of the Jordan as far as Beth Barah.
