# Lamentations 1:16 (New International Version)
"This is why I weep  
    and my eyes overflow with tears.[^A]  
No one is near to comfort[^B] me,  
    no one to restore my spirit.  
My children are destitute  
    because the enemy has prevailed."[^C]
