# Lamentations 1:4 (New International Version)
The roads to Zion mourn,[^A]  
    for no one comes to her appointed festivals.  
All her gateways are desolate,[^B]  
    her priests groan,  
her young women grieve,  
    and she is in bitter anguish.[^C]
