# Leviticus 16:16 (New International Version)
In this way he will make atonement[^A] for the Most Holy Place[^B] because of the uncleanness and rebellion of the Israelites, whatever their sins have been. He is to do the same for the tent of meeting,[^C] which is among them in the midst of their uncleanness.
