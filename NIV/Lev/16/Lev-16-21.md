# Leviticus 16:21 (New International Version)
He is to lay both hands on the head of the live goat[^A] and confess[^B] over it all the wickedness and rebellion of the Israelites--all their sins--and put them on the goat's head. He shall send the goat away into the wilderness in the care of someone appointed for the task.
