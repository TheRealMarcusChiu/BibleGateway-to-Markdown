# Leviticus 23:18 (New International Version)
Present with this bread seven male lambs, each a year old and without defect, one young bull and two rams. They will be a burnt offering to the LORD, together with their grain offerings and drink offerings[^A]--a food offering, an aroma pleasing to the LORD.
