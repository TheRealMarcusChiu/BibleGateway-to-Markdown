# Leviticus 4:18 (New International Version)
He is to put some of the blood[^A] on the horns of the altar that is before the LORD[^B] in the tent of meeting. The rest of the blood he shall pour out at the base of the altar[^C] of burnt offering at the entrance to the tent of meeting.
