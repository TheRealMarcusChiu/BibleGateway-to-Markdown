# Leviticus 5:16 (New International Version)
They must make restitution[^A] for what they have failed to do in regard to the holy things, pay an additional penalty of a fifth of its value[^B] and give it all to the priest. The priest will make atonement for them with the ram as a guilt offering, and they will be forgiven.
