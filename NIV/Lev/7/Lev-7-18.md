# Leviticus 7:18 (New International Version)
If any meat of the fellowship offering[^A] is eaten on the third day, the one who offered it will not be accepted.[^B] It will not be reckoned[^C] to their credit, for it has become impure; the person who eats any of it will be held responsible.[^D]
