# Mark 2:18 (New International Version)
Jesus Questioned About Fasting[^A]
Now John's disciples and the Pharisees were fasting.[^B] Some people came and asked Jesus, "How is it that John's disciples and the disciples of the Pharisees are fasting, but yours are not?"
