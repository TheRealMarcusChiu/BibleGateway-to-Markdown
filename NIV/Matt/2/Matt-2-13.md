# Matthew 2:13 (New International Version)
The Escape to Egypt
When they had gone, an angel[^A] of the Lord appeared to Joseph in a dream.[^B] "Get up," he said, "take the child and his mother and escape to Egypt. Stay there until I tell you, for Herod is going to search for the child to kill him."[^C]
