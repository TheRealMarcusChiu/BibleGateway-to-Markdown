# Micah 6:16 (New International Version)
You have observed the statutes of Omri[^A]  
    and all the practices of Ahab's[^B] house;  
    you have followed their traditions.[^C]  
Therefore I will give you over to ruin[^D]  
    and your people to derision;  
    you will bear the scorn[^E] of the nations."
