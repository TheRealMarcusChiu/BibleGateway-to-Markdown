# Nehemiah 11:1 (New International Version)
The New Residents of Jerusalem[^A]
Now the leaders of the people settled in Jerusalem. The rest of the people cast lots to bring one out of every ten of them to live in Jerusalem,[^B] the holy city,[^C] while the remaining nine were to stay in their own towns.[^D]
