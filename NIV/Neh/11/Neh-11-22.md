# Nehemiah 11:22 (New International Version)
The chief officer of the Levites in Jerusalem was Uzzi son of Bani, the son of Hashabiah, the son of Mattaniah,[^A] the son of Mika. Uzzi was one of Asaph's descendants, who were the musicians responsible for the service of the house of God.
