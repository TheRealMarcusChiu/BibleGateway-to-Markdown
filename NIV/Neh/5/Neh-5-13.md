# Nehemiah 5:13 (New International Version)
I also shook[^A] out the folds of my robe and said, "In this way may God shake out of their house and possessions anyone who does not keep this promise. So may such a person be shaken out and emptied!"
At this the whole assembly said, "Amen,"[^B] and praised the LORD. And the people did as they had promised.
