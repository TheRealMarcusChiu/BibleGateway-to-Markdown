# Nehemiah 5:14 (New International Version)
Moreover, from the twentieth year of King Artaxerxes,[^A] when I was appointed to be their governor[^B] in the land of Judah, until his thirty-second year--twelve years--neither I nor my brothers ate the food allotted to the governor.
