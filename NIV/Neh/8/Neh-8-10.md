# Nehemiah 8:10 (New International Version)
Nehemiah said, "Go and enjoy choice food and sweet drinks, and send some to those who have nothing[^A] prepared. This day is holy to our Lord. Do not grieve, for the joy[^B] of the LORD is your strength."
