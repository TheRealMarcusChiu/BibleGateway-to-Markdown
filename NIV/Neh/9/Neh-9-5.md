And the Levites--Jeshua, Kadmiel, Bani, Hashabneiah, Sherebiah, Hodiah, Shebaniah and Pethahiah--said: "Stand up and praise the LORD your God,[^A] who is from everlasting to everlasting."
"Blessed be your glorious name,[^B] and may it be exalted above all blessing and praise.
