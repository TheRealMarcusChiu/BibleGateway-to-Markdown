# Numbers 1:50 (New International Version)
Instead, appoint the Levites to be in charge of the tabernacle[^A] of the covenant law[^B]--over all its furnishings[^C] and everything belonging to it. They are to carry the tabernacle and all its furnishings; they are to take care of it and encamp around it.
