# Numbers 18:23 (New International Version)
It is the Levites who are to do the work at the tent of meeting and bear the responsibility for any offenses they commit against it. This is a lasting ordinance[^A] for the generations to come.[^B] They will receive no inheritance[^C] among the Israelites.[^D]
