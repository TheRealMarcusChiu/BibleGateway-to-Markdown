# Numbers 19:13 (New International Version)
If they fail to purify themselves after touching a human corpse,[^A] they defile the LORD's tabernacle.[^B] They must be cut off from Israel.[^C] Because the water of cleansing has not been sprinkled on them, they are unclean;[^D] their uncleanness remains on them.
