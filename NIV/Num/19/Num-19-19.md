# Numbers 19:19 (New International Version)
The man who is clean is to sprinkle[^A] those who are unclean on the third and seventh days, and on the seventh day he is to purify them.[^B] Those who are being cleansed must wash their clothes[^C] and bathe with water, and that evening they will be clean.
