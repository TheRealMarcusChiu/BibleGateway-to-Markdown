# Numbers 3:38 (New International Version)
Moses and Aaron and his sons were to camp to the east[^A] of the tabernacle, toward the sunrise, in front of the tent of meeting.[^B] They were responsible for the care of the sanctuary[^C] on behalf of the Israelites. Anyone else who approached the sanctuary was to be put to death.[^D]
