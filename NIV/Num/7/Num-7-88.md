# Numbers 7:88 (New International Version)
The total number of animals for the sacrifice of the fellowship offering[^A] came to twenty-four oxen, sixty rams, sixty male goats and sixty male lambs[^B] a year old. These were the offerings for the dedication of the altar after it was anointed.[^C]
