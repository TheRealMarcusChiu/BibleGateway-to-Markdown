# Obadiah 20 (New International Version)
This company of Israelite exiles who are in Canaan  
    will possess the land as far as Zarephath;[^A]  
the exiles from Jerusalem who are in Sepharad  
    will possess the towns of the Negev.[^B]

### Crossrefs
[^A]: 1Ki 17:9-10; Lk 4:26
[^B]: S Jer 33:13
