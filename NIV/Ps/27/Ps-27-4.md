# Psalm 27:4 (New International Version)
One thing[^A] I ask from the LORD,  
    this only do I seek:  
that I may dwell in the house of the LORD  
    all the days of my life,[^B]  
to gaze on the beauty of the LORD  
    and to seek him in his temple.
