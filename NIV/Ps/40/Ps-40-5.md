# Psalm 40:5 (New International Version)
Many, LORD my God,  
    are the wonders[^A] you have done,  
    the things you planned for us.  
None can compare[^B] with you;  
    were I to speak and tell of your deeds,  
    they would be too many[^C] to declare.
