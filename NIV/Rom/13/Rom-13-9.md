# Romans 13:9 (New International Version)
The commandments, "You shall not commit adultery," "You shall not murder," "You shall not steal," "You shall not covet,"[^A] and whatever other command there may be, are summed up[^B] in this one command: "Love your neighbor as yourself."[^C]
