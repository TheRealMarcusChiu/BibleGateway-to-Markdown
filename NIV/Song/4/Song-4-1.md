# Song of Songs 4:1 (New International Version)
How beautiful you are, my darling!  
    Oh, how beautiful!  
    Your eyes behind your veil[^A] are doves.[^B]  
Your hair is like a flock of goats  
    descending from the hills of Gilead.[^C]
