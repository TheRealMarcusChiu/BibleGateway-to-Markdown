# Song of Songs 8:6 (New International Version)
Place me like a seal over your heart,  
    like a seal on your arm;  
for love[^A] is as strong as death,  
    its jealousy[^B] unyielding as the grave.  
It burns like blazing fire,  
    like a mighty flame.
