# Zechariah 14:10 (New International Version)
The whole land, from Geba[^A] to Rimmon,[^B] south of Jerusalem, will become like the Arabah. But Jerusalem will be raised up[^C] high from the Benjamin Gate[^D] to the site of the First Gate, to the Corner Gate,[^E] and from the Tower of Hananel[^F] to the royal winepresses, and will remain in its place.[^G]
