# Zechariah 14:5 (New International Version)
You will flee by my mountain valley, for it will extend to Azel. You will flee as you fled from the earthquake[^A] in the days of Uzziah king of Judah. Then the LORD my God will come,[^B] and all the holy ones with him.[^C]
